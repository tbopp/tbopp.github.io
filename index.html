About

Hi, I'm Tyrell Bopp. I'm a full stack web developer and I absolutely love what I do, so I work hard to do it well. With over 7-years of experience working in Information Technology, I also like to think of myself as a continuous learner and professional problem solver. I'm deeply passionate about the synergy between people and technology and I live for the next challange to develop something great - and then try to make it even better!

From a very young age, I started developing serious interests in computers and coding. My interests were so innate that by the time I reached college, working with computers felt so second nature to me. I wanted to take the opportunity to try majoring in something that didn't feel so familiar and comfortable, so I pushed myself into majoring in English thinking one day I might like to teach.

I quickly realized my passions for technology never wavered and only grew stronger. Upon graduation, I immediately started a career in IT as a communications coordinator for a local textile manufacture. I was soon promoted to manager of technical training and support, and most recently, IT project manager. After years of working with developers, I knew it was time to follow my heart completely and pursue my dream job of being a developer. I'm thrilled to be here, and I can't wait to see how my unique expereince can be of value to you.

US Census Data Visualization

This web app allows users to search for any American city and view a data visualization of that city's demographics based on statistics from the United States Census Bureau. The US Census Bureau collects a plethora of data regarding America's demographics but the bureau doesn't always provide tools to easily navigate and analyze its datasets. Our project utilizes a comprehensive July 2009 dataset that hasn't previously been incorporated into any kind of data visualization tool.
